Steve Harrington is a fictional character from the Netflix television show Stranger Things, portrayed by Joe Keery. While starting out as a typical unlikable jock, Steve has grown into a more protecting and caring character as the show has progressed, a development that has received widespread acclaim from critics and fans alike and has led to him becoming one of the showâ€™s most beloved and enduring characters. Initially a part of the recurring cast, Keery was promoted to the main cast in the second season. Steve is a side antagonist turned protagonist at the end of season 1, and one of the main protagonists of season 2, season 3, and season 4.

Fictional Character Biography

Season 1

Steve Harrington starts as a popular high school student and boyfriend of Nancy Wheeler. He is initially portrayed as arrogant but later shows his caring side when he helps Nancy and Jonathan fight the Demogorgon.

Season 2

Steve takes on a more protective role, looking after Dustin and the other kids. He becomes a mentor figure and fights against the creatures from the Upside Down.

Season 3

Steve works at Scoops Ahoy with Robin Buckley and helps uncover the secret Russian operation beneath Starcourt Mall. He continues his role as a protector of the kids.

Season 4

Steve embarks on a mission into the Upside Down with Nancy, Robin, and others. He proves his bravery multiple times, further solidifying his role as a fan-favorite character.
